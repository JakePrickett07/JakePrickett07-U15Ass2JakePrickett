Our Event History

At our charity, we believe that Slovak culture should be experienced, not just learned. That's why we organise a variety of events for everyone, including the following:

    Families

    Children

    Adults

    Individuals who simply want to connect with Slovak heritage.

Some of the cultural experience, some come to practice their Slovak, and others just want to come and enjoy good company over coffee or a glass of wine. You are welcome.

A traditional Slovak carnival full of masks, costumes, music, and dancing. A fun oppoertunity for both children and adults to enjoy the vibrant and playful atmosphere of this centuries-old tradition.
